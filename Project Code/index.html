How everything works

Have clothes to sell?:

Tap the sell button or click here to go the the sell page. There you can fill out your information as well as all the information about the clothes you want to sell. Add some images of the product as well. Once you complete the form you may click submitt! That's it.

Evaluation

After your submission, you will recieve an email from us about your submission. We will evaluate your submission,making sure the product matches the description. We will then contact you and send you an adrress to drop off your item, afterwhich we will upload onto the website!

How are you getting paid?

Once your item gets sold on our page, we will inform you via email. The money initially goes into our account where we take 5% and then forward the balance to your account. It's as simple as that!
